About Me

I am the third year PhD student at Bordeaux University. I work in Image and Sound team at LaBRI. Projects

MAELab

A framework to automatize landmark estimation

MAELab is a open framework. The main aim of MAELab is provided the functions to automatize landmark in biology image, specify beetle images. Besides, it provides also the basic function in image processing such as segmentation, filtering, ....

MAELab is implemented by C++ with two distributed versions: library and graphic interface. The graphic interface of MAELab is build based on the Qt framework. This framework is during the construction.
